About Us

PYRAMID TECHNICAL SERVICES PVT. LTD. (PTS) incorporated in January 2014 as 100% subsidiary of Supreme Global Services Pvt. Ltd. (SGS) to focus on corrosion monitoring and prevention of oil and gas pipelines.

Welcome

The subsidiary company takes forward the good work done by the parent company in Oil and Gas industry in last 10 years. With corrosion dominating the oil and gas industry’s design and maintenance philosophy in the 21st century, PTS is trying to position itself in critical areas of prevention and monitoring.The directors of the company have vast experience of oil and gas industry and have executed projects worth more than USD 25 million in last 9 years.

The companies experience ranges from offshore corrosion monitoring to onshore civil works. Our current work profile includes: Corrosion Monitoring of Pipelines,Pigging Services,Turneky projects for construction of oil and gas facilities,In-Line Inspection of Pipelines.

Our Mission: To provide safe working environment and resource optimization to all stakeholders in oil and gas installations by active and passive monitoring and prevention of corrosion of pipelines.

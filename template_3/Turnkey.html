Turnkey Projects and ILI.

Our team of professionals is fully trained in operations of Turnkey Projects as well as ILI services for Oil and Gas Pipeleines.

    We undertake turnkey projects of construction/maintenance/repair and modification of piping, structural works, instrumentation works on offshore platforms as well as onshore installations such as Gas compressor stations. Our range of tools include dia from 10” up to 56” and we provide maximum possible accuracy in identifying pipeline defects

    We have executed overhauling of 210 pig traps by replacement of valves, trap closure and associated piping and in the process converted 115 lines of ONGC Mumbai offshore from non-piggable category to piggable category. We have executed piping work of up to 900 class.

    We provide following tool runs in identifying pipeline Defects:

  • Cleaning Pigs

  • EGP

  • Magnet Cleaning Pig

  • MFL, MFL+

  • TFI

  • EMAT

    • Our range of tool is able to identify all kind of internal and external pipeline defects including:

  • Metal loss due to corrosion

  • Weld anomalies

  • Girth Weld defects

  • Stress Corrosion Cracking

  • Hydrogen Induced Cracking

  • Pitting Corrosion

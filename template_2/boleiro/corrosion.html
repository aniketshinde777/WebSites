SERVICES

Corrosion Monitoring

Corrosion Monitoring is one of the basic needs of safe and efficient industrial operation especially hydrocarbon industry, chemical processing industry and power plants including nuclear power stations.

We are the leading provider of Corrosion Monitoring services in India. We provide turnkey solutions for corrosion monitoring of hydrocarbon pipelines by using LPR Probes, ER Probes and Weight Loss coupons, with facilities for training and data analysis.

Internal Corrosion Monitoring of Pipelines.

  • Monitoring of Internal Corrosion of Pipelines using LPR, ER Probes, Weight Loss Coupons, Bio Probes, Galvanic Probes, Sand Probes with Data Recording and Transmitting Equipment.

  • Team of highly skilled OEM certified personnel for high pressure installation and retrieval in oil and gas pipelines. Welder with extensive in-service welding experience and certified Hot-tapping technicians complete the Corrosion Monitoring team

  • Use of Fixed UT Probes for Corrosion Monitoring, Sand Erosion Monitoring; Acoustic Emission Probes for Leak Detection and Sand Monitoring; LRUT Probes for Pipeline Integrity Assessment; CP Monitoring of existing CP system and use it for intrusion and leak detection. Equipment available for high temperature applications in Refineries with logging as well as wireless communication system.

  • Benefits over conventional intrusive method :
    1. No welding, No Hot tapping, No maintenance
    2. Easy to install
    3. Direct Pipe wall Thickness monitoring
    4. Low cost and less manpower required.

Corrosion Monitoring using Non-Instrusive Methods

Corrosion Under Insulation.

  • Assessment of corrosion under insulation – pipes with aluminum cladding using non-intrusive technique for refineries, chemical plants, power plants by Magnetic Tomography.

  • Team of highly skilled OEM certified personnel for high pressure installation and retrieval in oil and gas pipelines. Welder with extensive in-service welding experience and certified Hot-tapping technicians complete the Corrosion Monitoring team

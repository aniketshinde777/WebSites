Integrity Survey of pipelines using MTM/AquaMTM

Integrity Assessment of Carbon Steel Pipelines with Non-Contact Magnetic Tomography method for both onshore and offshore buried pipelines.

Internal Corrosion Monitoring of Pipelines.

  • Advantage: A direct quantitative assessment of the stress-deformed condition of the pipe metal. Conventional surveys measure the geometrical parameters of a defect only, whereas MTM directly measures the stress caused by defects and thereby it is an inherently more accurate guide to the safe operation of the pipeline than conventional survey methods

  • Conventional surveys may fail to detect dangerously stressed areas of the pipe or, conversely, classify a defect as one which requires urgent attention when, in reality, the stress level may be low and the defect presents no immediate threat to the operation of the pipe.

  • The final report includes assessment of ERF, MAOP and FFP .
